CHOCOLATE SPIDERS

INGREDIENTS

  • Chang's Original Fried Noodles
  • 2 tablespoons crunchy peanut butter
  • 200g dark cooking chocolate

METHOD

  1. Microwave chocolate and peanut butter in microwave safe mixing bowl for about 30 seconds on high. If not melted, then microwave again in 5 second intervals. Mix well until it is a smooth paste.
  2. Add noodles and coat them well.
  3. Using heaped teaspoons spoon the mixture onto grease-proof paper, or into individual paper patty cases.
  4. Place in the refrigerator until set. The spiders can be kept in a sealed container in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
