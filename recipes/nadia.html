Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

  • 3 TB olive oil
  • 4 TB butter
  • 1.5 onion chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves chopped
  • 350gr mushrooms sliced
  • 300gr arborio rice
  • 600ml chicken broth
  • 200ml dry white wine
  • 220gr parmigiano-reggiano cheese grated
  • 150gr baby spinach cut in smaller pieces

Directions

  • Heat 3TBs olive oil and 2 Tbs butter in pressure cooker using "Brown" option
  • Add onion and brown for 3 minutes
  • Add garlic and brown for 1 minute
  • Add rice and brown for 2-3 minutes (until rice is coated in oil and onion)
  • Add mushrooms and saute for 1 minute
  • Add chicken broth and wine
  • Cover and cook on high pressure for 7 minutes
  • Release the pressure and add remaining 2 Tbs butter, parmesan and spinach
  • Garnish with cracked pepper and parsley
