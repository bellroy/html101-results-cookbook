Fluffy Gluten Free Scones

Fluffy Scones perfect with Jam and Cream for Afternoon Tea

Scones are a staple recipe we should all have in our arsenal. Light fluffy gluten free scones are a trusty recipe to have up your sleeve for when guests come over for afternoon tea.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gluten-free plain flour
  • ¼ cup white rice flour
  • ¼ cup almond meal
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • a good pinch of sea salt
  • 60ml cream
  • 125ml milk, plus a little extra for brushing
  • strawberry jam, to serve
  • whipped cream, to serve

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C/440°F/gas 7 and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Put the flours, almond meal, baking powder, and salt into a bowl. Make a well in the centre.
  3. Combine the cream and milk then pour in to the well and lightly mix just until the dough comes together. Andy's Nan (Nancy Nan)recommends stiring with a knife to keep the dough light.
  4. Lightly pat the dough out on a lightly floured surface and use a lightly floured round cutter, twist the cutter down to cut rounds out of the dough.
  5. Place the rounds touching each other into the lined baking tray, lightly the brush the tops with milk and bake for 10 minutes, or until risen and golden.
  6. Break in half whilst warm and serve with jam and whipped cream.
