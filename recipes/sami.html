Sami's Christmas Ham

  • 5kg Cooked Leg Ham
  • 2 Tbsp Whole Cloves
  • 1 Can Guinness Beer
  • 1 Cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 Cup of Honey
  • 3/4 Cup Whole Grain Mustard

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees.
  2. Using a sharp knife, make an incision around the shank of the ham.
  3. Starting at the bottom of the ham, slide your fingers under the skin and work off the fat from the top to the shank. Discard skin.
  4. Using a sharp knife, score shallow criss-cross patterns in fat at 1.5cm intervals, without cutting into meat.
  5. Stud the criss-cross patterns with a clove in each diamond.
  6. On stove-top, combine Guinness Beer, Maple Syrup, Honey and Mustard. Stir until simmering.
  7. Glaze ham with combined ingredients. Bake for 2 hours, basting ham at 20 minute intervals.
  8. Slice and serve.
